About

Modernizing School Communication

Our mission

We believe that technology should simplify, not complicate. That's why we created Comtro – a smart announcement and bell automation system that puts powerful communication tools in the palm of your hand. Founded by educators and technologists, we understand the daily challenges schools face. Our platform is designed to save time, reduce complexity, and ensure that important messages always reach the right people at the right time.

Our priorities
